## THE "CLEVER TRAP" EXPLAINED

The model works because:

1. **GC has no cash value** — 100% margin, pure virtual currency
2. **$C is FREE** — given as bonus, not purchased, avoids gambling classification
3. **Consumables attach** — 25% of GC spent on high-margin digital goods
4. **Redemption friction** — 10% fee + play-through requirement reduces cashouts
5. **Churn works FOR you** — 60% of $C never gets redeemed (played back into house edge)

**Result:** $20 in → $20+ profit while player feels they got $22 value.
