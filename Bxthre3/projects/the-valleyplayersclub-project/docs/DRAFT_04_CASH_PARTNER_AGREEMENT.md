VPC appoints Partner as a non-exclusive independent contractor to:
- Accept physical cash deposits from authorized VPC Players
- Verify Player identity via Deposit Request token
- Process deposits through VPC's mobile Partner application
- Maintain Secured Collateral as specified herein
- Execute Cash Drops per schedule

### 2.2 Non-Exclusive Relationship

Partner acknowledges:
- VPC may engage other Partners in same geographic area
- Partner may not accept deposits for competing sweepstakes platforms
- VPC may terminate this Agreement without cause on 30 days notice

### 2.3 No Agency

Partner is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, or representative of VPC. Partner has no authority to:
- Bind VPC to any obligation
- Modify VPC's terms, policies, or pricing
- Accept any liability on VPC's behalf
- Act as money transmitter (Partner acts solely as VPC's limited payment agent)

## 4. DEPOSIT PROCESS

### 4.1 Deposit Request Generation

Player generates Deposit Request via VPC platform:
- Unique alphanumeric token (e.g., VPC-CASH-XXXXXX)
- QR code encoding token
- Expires 24 hours from generation
- Single-use only

### 4.2 In-Person Verification

Partner shall verify in presence of Player:

| Step | Verification | Method |
|------|------------|--------|
| 1 | Token validity | Scan QR or enter code in Partner App |
| 2 | Token expiration | System timestamp check |
| 3 | Player identity | Match photo on Player's government ID to VPC account photo |
| 4 | Amount | Confirm amount matches Player statement |
| 5 | Physical cash | Count cash in presence of Player; provide receipt |

### 4.3 Confirmation

Upon successful verification:
- Partner logs deposit in Partner App
- System immediately credits Player's VPC account with equivalent GC
- Player receives SMS/email confirmation
- Secured Collateral "available balance" reduced by deposit amount

### 4.4 Prohibited Practices

Partner shall NOT:
- Accept deposits without valid token
- Accept deposits exceeding available Secured Collateral
- Accept third-party deposits (must match VPC account holder)
- Split large deposits to avoid thresholds
- Accept deposits outside approved business hours without VPC authorization
- Provide change or cash back for deposits
- Accept deposits while "frozen" or "suspended"

## 5. CASH DROP & RECONCILIATION

### 5.1 Drop Schedule

Partner selects schedule during onboarding:

| Type | Trigger | Max Holding Period |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Daily | Business close | 24 hours |
| Threshold | $500 accumulated | Until threshold reached |
| Scheduled | Specific days/times | 7 days maximum |

Partner may modify schedule with 48-hour advance notice to VPC.

### 5.2 Drop Execution

1. Partner prepares cash for deposit
2. Partner transports to VPC-designated bank location
3. Partner presents QR code from Partner App
4. Bank teller verifies code, counts cash, provides receipt
5. System updates: Secured Collateral "available balance" restored
6. GPS verification and photo timestamp confirm drop

### 5.3 Reconciliation Discrepancies

| Scenario | Resolution | Liability |
|----------|------------|-----------|
| Partner records $500; bank receives $450 | Partner liable for $50 | Charged against Secured Collateral |
| Partner records $500; bank receives $550 | VPC credits Partner $50 | Partner net +$50 |
| Cash lost in transit | Insurance claim; Partner liable for deductible | Secured Collateral charged |

Discrepancy resolution: Partner has 24 hours to dispute with supporting evidence.

## 6. COMMISSION & COMPENSATION

### 6.1 Commission Calculation

Partner earns commission on successfully processed deposits:

**Formula:** Commission = Deposit Amount × Tier Rate

**Example (Silver Tier - 1.5%):**
- Player deposits $100 → Partner earns $1.50
- Player deposits $50 → Partner earns $0.75

### 6.2 Commission Payment

- **Frequency:** Monthly, 15 days following month-end
- **Method:** ACH to Partner's verified bank account or USDC to verified wallet
- **Minimum:** $25.00 (amounts below rolled to next period)
- **Reporting:** Monthly statement with per-transaction detail

### 6.3 Chargebacks & Adjustments

If Player payment reversed (chargeback, fraud):
- Commission clawed back from future payments
- If insufficient future commissions, Secured Collateral charged
- Partner may dispute if VPC error, not Partner verification failure

## 7. BURN EVENT & COLLATERAL FORFEITURE

### 7.1 Burn Triggers

Secured Collateral burned (forfeited to VPC) upon:

| Event | Burn Amount | Evidence Required |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| Partner theft/embezzlement | Full collateral | Police report, investigation |
| Unexplained cash shortage >10% | Shortage amount | Audit report, bank records |
| Fraudulent deposit processing | Deposit amount | Token logs, KYC mismatch |
| Regulatory enforcement | VPC discretion | Citation, order |
| Material breach | VPC discretion | Breach notice, cure period expiration |

### 7.2 Burn Procedure

1. VPC detects trigger event
2. VPC freezes Partner account (no new deposits)
3. VPC provides written notice with 48-hour response period
4. Partner may dispute with documentary evidence
5. VPC Compliance Officer makes determination
6. If Burn confirmed:
   - Digital collateral transferred to VPC operating account
   - Partner receives burn notice with calculation
   - Remaining collateral (if any) returned to Partner
7. Partner may appeal to arbitration within 30 days

### 7.3 80% Freeze Rule

When Partner's "available" Secured Collateral (collateral minus held cash) falls below 80% of maximum capacity:
- New deposits automatically blocked
- Partner must execute Cash Drop or deposit additional collateral
- Automatic Burn of full remaining collateral if not resolved within 72 hours

**DRAFTING ATTORNEY NOTES FOR REVIEW:**

1. **Money Transmission Analysis:** Confirm Partner is not functioning as unlicensed money transmitter. Structure should be "payment agent" not "transmitter." Reference to MoneyGram v. FTC analysis.

2. **UCC Article 9 Compliance:** Verify security interest perfection requirements in Wyoming and Partner's state. Digital collateral (USDC) may require "control" under revised Article 9.

3. **Employee vs. Independent Contractor:** Classification must be ironclad. Partner cannot be directed as employee (hours, methods, exclusivity). Review IRS 20-factor test.

4. **Burn Event Due Process:** 48-hour response period + appeal to arbitration provides adequate due process. Confirm enforceability under Wyoming law.

5. **Insurance Requirements:** Crime/employee dishonesty coverage is critical given cash handling. Verify $100K minimum is actuarially sound.

6. **AML Training Obligation:** Partner training creates vicarious liability if Partner fails. Document training completion rigorously.

7. **Jurisdictional Variance:** Some states (CA, NY) have additional independent contractor protections. May need addendum for Partners in those states.

8. **Arbitration Enforceability:** FAA preemption applies. Class waiver enforceable under *AT&T Mobility v. Concepcion* but verify current 9th Circuit (if applicable) stance.
