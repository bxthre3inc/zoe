## 1. DEFINITIONS & DUAL CURRENCY SYSTEM

### 1.1 Gold Coins ("GC")
**DRAFTING NOTE:** *Distinguishing play money from redeemable currency is essential for sweepstakes classification. See FTC v. Reader's Digest (1996).*

"Gold Coins" or "GC" are virtual currency with no monetary value. GC:
- Cannot be redeemed for cash or any item of value
- Are used solely to access free-to-play games for entertainment
- May be purchased via credit card, debit card, or other payment methods
- Purchases of GC are final and non-refundable except as required by law
- GC have no cash value and do not expire while account remains active

### 1.2 Sweeps Credits ("$C")
**DRAFTING NOTE:** *"Sweeps Coins" is industry standard terminology. Using "$C" with dollar sign is intentional branding. Must explicitly state "no purchase necessary" — this is the legal hook.*

"Sweeps Credits" or "$C" are promotional sweepstakes entries that:
- Have no cash value until redeemed in accordance with these Terms
- Are earned through gameplay, promotional offers, or Alternative Method of Entry (AMOE)
- May be redeemed for USD at the exchange rate of 1 $C = $1.00 USD
- Are subject to a 10.009% processing fee upon redemption
- Require minimum 10,000 $C balance for redemption ($10.00 USD minimum)
- Require minimum 1x playthrough (wagered at least once) before redemption
- Require successful KYC/AML verification before redemption processing

**IMPORTANT:** You do NOT need to purchase GC to receive $C. See Section 4 (AMOE).

### 1.3 Secured Cash Partner Network
**DRAFTING NOTE:** *This is unique to VPC. Needs clear liability shield. Reference Square Cash merchant model.*

"Cash Partners" are independent third-party businesses authorized to:
- Accept physical cash deposits from Players
- Credit Player accounts with equivalent GC (not $C)

- Process verified cash drops at designated banking partners

VPC is not a money transmitter. Cash Partners operate under independent contractor agreements with UCC-1 secured interest and digital collateral requirements.
