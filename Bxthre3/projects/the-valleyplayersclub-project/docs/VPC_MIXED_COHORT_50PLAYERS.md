## KEY INSIGHTS

1. **$C Churn is #1 profit driver** — 35% of profit from unclaimed sweepstakes credits
2. **Consumables = 26%** — high-margin digital goods
3. **House edge only 4%** — surprisingly small compared to other sources
4. **25 whales = 4x revenue of 25 regulars** — but cost structure similar
