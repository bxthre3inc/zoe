## KEY INSIGHTS

### 1. MEMBERSHIPS = PREDICTABLE REVENUE
- **$18,168/year** guaranteed before anyone plays a game
- 33% of total revenue is subscription-based
- Churn < 5% for Gold/Platinum (they're committed)

### 2. HIGHER TIERS = BETTER CHURN ECONOMICS
| Tier | Churn | Profit/Player |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Bronze | 40% | $30 |
| Platinum | 50% | $301 |

*Wait — Platinum has HIGHER churn but MORE profit?*

**YES:** Because they spend **10x more** on consumables and GC. The $C is just a loss leader.

### 3. THE CONSUMABLES GOLDMINE
- Membership players buy **3x more** consumables
- Why? XP boosts, de-level protection, RTP bonuses
- These have **100% margin** (digital goods)

### 4. LIFETIME VALUE (LTV)

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | 2-Year LTV |
|------|---------|--------|------------|
| Bronze | $30 | $361 | $722 |
| Silver | $74 | $887 | $1,775 |
| Gold | $156 | $1,869 | $3,738 |
| Platinum | $301 | $3,610 | $7,219 |

**Acquire a Platinum for $500? You profit $6,700 over 2 years.**
