## 6. BIOMETRIC DATA (BIPA COMPLIANCE)

**DRAFTING NOTE:** *Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act requires specific disclosures.*

If you complete KYC verification, we collect biometric data (facial geometry) via liveness detection.

**Required Disclosures:**
- **Collection:** One-time capture during KYC
- **Purpose:** Identity verification, fraud prevention
- **Storage:** Encrypted, access-controlled database
- **Retention:** 3 years from collection or account closure
- **Destruction:** Secure deletion after retention period
- **No Sale:** We never sell, lease, or trade biometric data

**Consent:** By completing KYC, you consent to biometric data collection as described above.

**Rights (Illinois Residents):**
- Right to know if biometric data is being collected
- Right to know purpose and duration
- Right to written release (this policy constitutes release)
- Private right of action for BIPA violations ($1,000-$5,000 per violation)
