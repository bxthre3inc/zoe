# FarmSense IP Securing Strategy & Budget

To protect the multi-million dollar valuation of FarmSense prior to deploying physical hardware into the CSU SLV RC fields or signing DoD testing agreements, the Intellectual Property (IP) must be legally secured.

This IP budget is split into two strategic phases to optimize for cash flow:

* **Phase 1 (Pre-Pilot):** Filing US Provisional Patent Applications (PPAs). This instantly legally secures a 12-month Priority Date and grants the "Patent Pending" status required to safely talk to investors, CSU, and grant agencies.
* **Phase 2 (Post-Pilot Validation):** Converting the PPAs into full Non-Provisional (Utility) Patents and filing international PCTs (Patent Cooperation Treaty) using the million-dollar grant money won *after* the pilot succeeds.

---

## The 6 Core Patentable Claims

FarmSense has developed a massive, defensible patent portfolio. We will file 6 distinct PPAs to create a "picket fence" of IP that prevents competitors from designing around us:

1. **Distributed Ledger Water Accounting (AllianceChain):** The method of using PBFT (Practical Byzantine Fault Tolerance) consensus over low-bandwidth LoRa FHSS for immutable agricultural water tracking.
2. **Privacy-Preserving Spatial Modeling (Edge FHE):** The method of combining Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) with geospatial Kriging interpolation at the edge.
3. **Sensor Deployment Mechanics (AKP-LRZ):** The airborne kinetic penetrator shock-absorption and deployment methodology for dropping sensors from UAVs.
4. **Zero-Cavitation Sleds:** The mechanical "friction-forming" manufacturing process and geometries of HDPE/PVC driving tips that prevent soil cracking upon insertion.
5. **Pivot Auditor Data Fusion:** The algorithm for fusing ultrasonic/paddle flow-state changes with high-frequency RTK GNSS spectral vibration to perfectly deduce center pivot irrigation rates.
6. **Universal Actuation Abstraction:** The abstraction layer translating standard field prescriptions into VRI (Variable Rate Irrigation) and ZVRI (Zone VRI) motor commands across disparate legacy pivot platforms (Lindsay, Valley, Reinke).
