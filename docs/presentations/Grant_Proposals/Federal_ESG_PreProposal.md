# Federal ESG Pre-Proposal: FarmSense Digital Water Ledger

**Executive Summary**
FarmSense is a deterministic agricultural intelligence platform designed to eliminate "black box" AI in water management. By integrating high-fidelity spatial sensors (SDR9 standard) with a hardware-integrated PBFT blockchain (AllianceChain), FarmSense provides a trustless, immutable ledger for water rights trading and consumption verification in critical basins like the San Luis Valley.

## 1. Technical Architecture & Innovation

### 1.1 Sensor Hierarchy (The "Truth" Model)

Our architecture utilizes a multi-tiered node system to ensure spatial and vertical fidelity:

- **LRZ1 (Lateral Root-Zone) "Truth Node"**: Monolithic, high-density units providing the permanent spatial ground truth.
- **VFA (Vertical Field Anchor) "Foundation Node"**: Seasonal Sled/Shell units optimized for deep vertical moisture profiling.
- **LRZ2 (Lateral Root-Zone) "Reference Node"**: Cost-effective Sled/Shell units for high-resolution spatial variability mapping.

### 1.2 Deterministic Engine (No Black Box AI)

Unlike probabilistic models, FarmSense utilizes an **Adaptive Recalculation Engine** based on "Fisherman's Attention." This engine dynamically prioritizes compute resources when critical events occur (e.g., rapid moisture depletion or pump failure), ensuring real-time response without the unpredictability of generative models.

## 2. Empirical Metrics & Environmental Impact

### 2.1 Water Savings

Through high-resolution moisture mapping (1m² grid), FarmSense enables:

- **Variable Rate Irrigation (VRI)**: Up to 30% reduction in water usage.
- **Evapotranspiration (ET) Precision**: Real-time adjustment based on local canopy temperature and wind gradients.

### 2.2 Immutable Governance (AllianceChain)

Integrated PBFT consensus ensures:

- **Non-Repudiation**: Water trades are finalized at the edge before internet uplink.
- **Transparency**: A public, verifiable ledger for subdistrict-level compliance.

## 3. Deployment Plan: SLV Subdistrict 1

- **Pilot Phase**: Deployment across 5,000 acres in Q2 2026.
- **Hardware Standard**: All buried components utilize **HDPE SDR9** for legacy-grade durability in pressurized agricultural environments.
