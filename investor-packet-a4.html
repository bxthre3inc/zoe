12. GLOSSARY & INDEX

A

Adaptive Recalculation Engine — Dynamic computation scheduler. Modes: DORMANT, ANTICIPATORY, FOCUS RIPPLE, FOCUS COLLAPSE.

AES-256 — Advanced Encryption Standard. Used for all field telemetry.

AllianceChain — FarmSense’s PBFT consensus protocol for Digital Water Ledger.

Alpha-Sled — Removable electronics cartridge deployed seasonally.

ANTICIPATORY Mode — Engine state triggered by rapid temperature rise.

Aquifer — Underground geological formation containing groundwater.

B

Black Box Cache — 128GB SSD in DHU for 30-day encrypted audit recording.

BLE 5.0 — Bluetooth Low Energy for technician maintenance access.

BOM — Bill of Materials. Complete parts list with costs.

C

CSS LoRa — Chirp Spread Spectrum LoRa at 915MHz. Primary telemetry protocol.

CSA — Corner-Swing Arm. BLE 5.2 distance ranging device.

COLLAPSE Mode — Critical engine state during active irrigation.

Cokriging — Multivariate geostatistical interpolation.

Core Compute Server (Zo) — Cloud-based scientific engine.

Crop Coefficient (Kc) — Factor relating actual ET to reference ET.

D

DHU — District Hub. Level 2 infrastructure node. 35’ pole, Orin Nano, 100-pivot radius.

Digital Water Ledger (DWL) — Cryptographically signed chain of custody.

DIL — Data Integration Layer.

DORMANT Mode — Low-power engine state. 4-hour polling, 8µA draw.

E

Edge-EBK — Empirical Bayesian Kriging at PMT. 50m resolution.

ESP32-S3 — MCU in PMT. Dual-core, vector FPU, LoRa.

ET (Evapotranspiration) — Combined water loss from soil + plants.

F

FOCUS RIPPLE — Engine state triggered by >5% moisture deviation.

G

GNSS — Global Navigation Satellite System. Sub-cm positioning.

GroPoint Profile — Commercial soil moisture sensor in VFA.

H

HDPE SDR9 — High-Density Polyethylene pipe for VFA/LRZ shells.

Hydraulic Conductivity (Ksat) — Rate of water movement through saturated soil.

I

IMU — Inertial Measurement Unit. 9-axis for tower stability.

ISLAND Mode — PMT autonomous operation when DHU offline.

K

Kc — See: Crop Coefficient.

Kriging — Geostatistical interpolation. FarmSense uses Ordinary, Regression, Edge-EBK.

L

LiFePO4 — Lithium Iron Phosphate battery. DHU, PFA.

LiSOCl2 — Lithium Thionyl Chloride. LRZ1/LRZ2 (10-year life).

LoRa — Long Range low-power wireless. 915MHz CSS LoRa.

LRZ1/LRZ2 — Lateral Root-Zone Basic/Reference. $29/$54 spatial scouts.

LTE-M — Low-power cellular for DHU backhaul fallback.

M

MAD — Management Allowable Depletion. % of available water before crop damage.

MAPE — Mean Absolute Percentage Error. Kriging validation target <5%.

N

NREP — Non-Repudiable Evidence Prime. Legal admissibility standard.

P

PBFT — Practical Byzantine Fault Tolerance. Consensus algorithm.

PMT — Pivot Motion Tracker. Field hub with edge-EBK engine.

PFA — Pressure & Flow Anchor. Flow measurement node.

PSLC — Pseudo-SLC flash. High-endurance SSD in DHU.

R

RFM95W — 915MHz LoRa radio module.

RSS — Regional Superstation. 40ft container, Threadripper, 50TB.

RTK — Real-Time Kinematic. Sub-cm GNSS positioning.

S

SDR9 — Standard Dimension Ratio 9. HDPE pipe specification.

SLV — San Luis Valley. Colorado pilot location.

SPAC — Soil-Plant-Atmosphere Continuum. Thermodynamic model.

T

TAM — Total Addressable Market. FarmSense TAM: $19-44B/year.

TimescaleDB — PostgreSQL extension for time-series data.

V

VFA — Vertical Field Anchor. 48" deep-profile truth node.

VRI — Variable Rate Irrigation. Prescription-based water application.
