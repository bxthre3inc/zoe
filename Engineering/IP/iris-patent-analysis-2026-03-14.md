## 1. Sagri Co., Ltd. — PCT/JP2025/029027

**Filing:** October 2025 (International Patent under PCT)  
**Tech Stack:** Satellite data + IoT sensors + Model-based forecasting  
**Focus:** Paddy field water level management

### Key Claims
- Proprietary water level forecasting combined with real-time satellite observation
- Ground-based IoT sensor integration
- Process-based modeling for rice cultivation
- Target markets: Philippines, Vietnam, Cambodia (SE Asia expansion)

### FarmSense Overlap Assessment: **MODERATE-HIGH**
| Component | Sagri | FarmSense |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Satellite data | ✓ Sentinel/other | ✓ Multi-source |
| IoT sensors | ✓ Ground-based | ✓ Sensor suites |
| Predictive models | ✓ Process-based | ✓ Deterministic |
| Crop focus | Paddy/rice | Multi-crop |
| Geography | SE Asia | Global |

### Risk Factors
- **PCT filing** = seeking protection in 150+ countries
- Core tech (satellite+IoT+model) identical to FarmSense stack
- Different crop focus (rice vs. multi-crop) may limit direct conflict
- Prior art search recommended before FarmSense filings in rice/paddy space

## 2. POWEIr Controller (Academic Research)

**Publication:** Applied Energy, 2025  
**Authors:** Research group (university/academic)  
**Tech Stack:** Predictive optimal water and energy irrigation  
**Focus:** Solar-powered drip irrigation in resource-constrained contexts

### Key Differentiators
- **Energy optimization** primary focus (battery + solar scheduling)
- Drip irrigation (not broad acre/paddy)
- Academic research (not commercial product yet)
- Resource-constrained deployment focus

### FarmSense Overlap Assessment: **LOW**
- Different irrigation methodology
- Energy management not FarmSense primary focus
- Academic vs. commercial positioning
- No satellite data component mentioned
